Common Issues Customers Face

At Bank of America, we understand that banking issues can be frustrating. Here are some common problems customers report:

  • Account Login Issues: Trouble accessing online or mobile banking accounts.
  • Unauthorized Transactions: Unexpected charges or suspicious account activity.
  • Card Declines: Debit or credit cards being declined unexpectedly.
  • Payment Delays: Delayed fund transfers or bill payments.
  • ATM Errors: Issues withdrawing cash or ATM machine malfunctions.
  • Customer Support Wait Times: Long hold times when calling support.

If you are facing any of these issues, call us immediately for assistance or visit our Help Center for step-by-step troubleshooting guides.
